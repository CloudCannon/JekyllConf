Details

Where is JekyllConf being held?

JekyllConf is a virtual conference. We're using Google Hangouts on Air to stream the speaker sessions straight to this website.

I can't attend, will the videos be available later?

Yes, all the videos will be publicly available on YouTube.

Who's running JekyllConf and why?

CloudCannon is organising JekyllConf. We want to help grow the Jekyll community and showcase all the awesome people and projects using Jekyll.
