Who's running JekyllConf and why?

JekyllConf is being put on by CloudCannon. We make a platform which allows developers to create websites in HTML or Jekyll and have non-technical people make updates inline. We want to do our part in growing the community and showcase all the awesome people and projects using Jekyll.
