Yes, all the videos will be publicly available on YouTube after the event.

Who's running JekyllConf and why?

CloudCannon is organising JekyllConf. We make a platform which allows developers to create websites in HTML or Jekyll and have non-technical people make updates inline. We want to do our part in growing the community and showcase all the awesome people and projects using Jekyll.
